सुरReal (Solo Singing Competition)

Description:

A massive platform to believe in yourself and bring out the melody in you. Grab this opportunity to fascinate everyone with your immersive performance and let the souls of the audience feel immersed in the depth of your song.

Steps to apply:

  1. Interested Vocalists must submit a demo in mp4 format.
  2. Top nine entries will be shortlisted for onstage performance.

Deadline:

5th November’2019

Rules: (For onstage performance)

  1. During the event, the vocalist may be accompanied by (at maximum) one instrumentalist which means at maximum one instrument is allowed per performance.
  2. You need to perform two songs.
  3. Performers will have 10 mins on stage(including setup and soundcheck).
  4. You must bring your own instruments and cables. (technical support will be provided in case of any emergency).
  5. NO backing tracks allowed.
  6. Pedalling, looping, drumming is strictly prohibited.

Judging Criteria:

  1. The winner will be chosen by a three-judge panel. The panel will critique the performance and provide feedback with recommendations.
  2. Vocalist-cum-instrumentalist will have an added advantage over vocalist accompanied by the instrumentalist.
