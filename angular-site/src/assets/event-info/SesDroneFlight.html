Drone Game

Objective: 

To fly a radio controlled drone from point A to point B through a route consisting of obstacles and checkpoints.

 

Structure:

The arena will consist of 5 circular checkpoints at different heights.The drone must fly through all of them to complete the challenge.
There may be obstacles between two checkpoints.

 

Time Penalty : 

Everytime a drone touches ground, A penalty of +20 seconds will added.

In case drone touches the outer ring of checkpoint penalty of 10 seconds will be added.

If a drone misses the checkpoint it should fly back and complete that checkpoint. 

 

Winner: 

The participant with least time at the end of the day will be considered winner and will be awarded a drone.
